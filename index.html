“The ORA-X is a cutting edge wearable device category that enables a brand new user experience. It is the world’s 1st smart headphones.”

The ORA-X seamlessly blends audio with a new visual experience. It will revolutionize the headphone and mobile computing space. It runs Android and will feature high fidelity audio sound as well as a disruptive transparent retinal projection technology for crisp and bright image display.

What defines the ORA-X

Novel User Experience

  • Audio and video content in one slick device.

  • Patented transparent augmented reality display developed over several years of research and development. This innovation allows the wearer to see digital information or video content without blocking his vision.

  • High fidelity audio with superior components for crystal clear sound quality and deep bass.

  • Runs Android applications as a powerful stand-alone device with on board microprocessor, memory, microphone, camera, capacitive touchpad, and sensors (gyro, compass, accelerometer, proximity, GPS).

Design

Beautifully designed sophisticated headphones with a retractable AR display. High end materials like machined metal and leather accents for robustness and high build quality. The capability of having a “wearable tablet” while looking stylish. Comfortable, ergonomic and mobile.

Mobile and Convenient

  • Take your content
    wherever you go

  • Information / notifications / navigation on the go while enjoying music (no need to take out and look down at phone)

  • Fully wireless
    (Wi-Fi and BT enabled)

  • High capacity battery
    lasts all day long

What makes the ORA-X different

High Fidelity Crystal Clear Audio

  • High end 50mm drivers for full rich base response.
  • Superior high definition audio codec using professional grade DSP technology.
  • Noise Isolation using high density memory foam for an immersive audio experience.
  • “Pass through” mode using integrated microphone to be aware of surrounding environment:
    • User can select between noise isolation and pass through mode.
  • Seamlessly sync with any Bluetooth 3.0/4.0 compatible devices.

Form Factor

  • Headphones have evolved very little. The ORA-X takes this to the next level.
  • Two modes: Traditional headphones or smart AR headphones: ORA-X has an adjustable display and camera that can be pivoted up and out of the way.
  • A beautiful pair of high end headphones with a revolutionary "extra feature".
